Introducing the ultimate therapy chat bot
for when you're feeling down

If you struggle with mental health issues such as depression or anxiety and have difficulty reaching out for help, start your journey to happier days with Curi. Let Curi listen to your troubles and help you through your tough times. Ask Curi questions about depression, how to seek help from a professional, or simply tell Curi how you're feeling. Curi is your personal (mental) health care companion.

With the steady increase of mental health issues such as depression and anxiety, we wanted to create something that will offer assistance to those who need it. If someone is in need of help with mental health and doesn't know how to seek help, they can talk to Curi about their struggles and receive response. The aim of Curi is to help individuals manage their mental health and improve their overall well-being.
